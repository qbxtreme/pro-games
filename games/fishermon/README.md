# Fishermon

**Hub ID:** `fishermon`  
**Path:** `games/fishermon/`  
**Play:** [http://localhost:8080/games/fishermon/](http://localhost:8080/games/fishermon/)

## Overview

Full custom fishing RPG inspired by All Out’s Fishermon. Catch and collect fish across zones, upgrade rods, hunt mythic bosses, earn pearls, and PvP rivals. Travel between zones by walking to dock ends and **⛵ Sail** (no warp portals).

## Save & multiplayer

- **Save key:** `fishermon`
- **MP room:** `fishermon` · zone-based subrooms

## Core loops

- Fish in zone waters; sell catches for coins
- Upgrade rods, bait, cooler, anchor, net (Pro Max Gear maxes these)
- Boss hunts and pearl economy
- **Worlds** menu for zone selection
